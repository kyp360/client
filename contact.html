Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries

What are the admission requirements for your courses?

Admission requirements vary by course level and subject. Generally, we require a completed application form, previous academic transcripts, and sometimes a preliminary assessment. For advanced courses, specific prerequisites might be necessary. We recommend checking the specific course page for detailed requirements or contacting our admissions team.

Do you offer scholarships or financial aid?

Yes, Goodfluencers offers various scholarships and financial aid options to qualified students. These include merit-based scholarships, need-based financial aid, and special grants for exceptional circumstances. We also offer flexible payment plans to help make education more accessible. Contact our financial aid office for more information on eligibility criteria and application procedures.

Can I switch courses after enrollment?

Yes, students can request to switch courses within the first two weeks of the program start date without additional fees. After this period, course changes are subject to availability and may involve administrative fees. You'll need to fill out a course change request form and meet with an academic advisor to ensure the new course aligns with your academic goals.

Are there any online courses available?

Absolutely! We offer a comprehensive range of online courses that mirror our in-person curriculum. These courses provide the same high-quality education with additional flexibility for distance learning. Our online platform includes interactive lectures, discussion forums, virtual labs, and one-on-one sessions with instructors. Check our Courses page and look for the "Online" tag to explore all available online options.

How can I request a transcript or certificate?

You can request official transcripts and certificates through our Student Portal or by submitting a request form to the Registrar's Office. Digital copies are typically processed within 2-3 business days, while physical copies may take 5-7 business days plus delivery time. There is a small administrative fee for each document requested. For urgent requests, we also offer expedited processing options.
