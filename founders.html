Meet Our Founders

Visionaries who are transforming education through innovation and excellence

Dr. Emily Carter

Co-Founder & Chief Academic Officer

Dr. Emily Carter is an award-winning educator with over 15 years of experience in academic leadership and pedagogical innovation. With a Ph.D. in Educational Psychology from Stanford University and a background in curriculum development, she brings a wealth of knowledge to Goodfluencers.

Prior to founding Goodfluencers, Dr. Carter served as the Director of Innovative Learning at the National Education Research Institute, where she led initiatives focusing on personalized learning approaches and technology integration in education.

Professor James Wilson

Co-Founder & Chief Innovation Officer

Professor James Wilson is a renowned technologist and education reformer who believes in the power of blending traditional teaching methods with cutting-edge technology. With an MSc in Computer Science from MIT and a background in AI research, James brings a unique perspective to educational methodologies.

Before co-founding Goodfluencers, Professor Wilson pioneered several EdTech startups and served as a consultant for major educational institutions worldwide. His innovative approaches to learning have been featured in leading publications including Education Today and Tech & Learning magazine.

The Goodfluencers Journey

2018

The Beginning

Dr. Carter and Professor Wilson first meet at the International Education Technology Conference in San Francisco, where they discover their shared vision for transforming education.

2019

The Vision Takes Shape

After months of research and planning, they develop the core methodology that would become the foundation of Goodfluencers' teaching approach.

2020

Goodfluencers is Born

Goodfluencers officially launches with its first batch of students and a small team of carefully selected educators committed to excellence.

2021

Rapid Growth

Following remarkable success rates and growing demand, Goodfluencers expands its course offerings and opens two additional branches.

2023

Innovation & Recognition

Goodfluencers receives the prestigious Education Innovation Award and launches its proprietary learning platform combining AI and personalized coaching.

2025

The Future

With a growing community of successful alumni and continued innovation, Goodfluencers is poised to revolutionize education on a global scale.
